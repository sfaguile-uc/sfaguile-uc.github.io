Sergio Aguilera is an Assistant Professor at the Department of Mechanical Engineering at the Pontificia Universidad Católica de Chile. He leads the Robotic Dynamic Interactions (RoDI) Laboratory. His group focuses on developing algorithms that enable robots to operate safely in dynamic environments while interacting with objects under uncertainty. Sergio received his Ph.D. in Robotics from Georgia Institute of Technology in 2023, received his B.Sc. and M.Sc in Electrical Engineering from Pontificia Universidad Católica de Chile in 2012 and 2014, respectively. Before becoming an assistant Professor, he worked as a robotics research engineer at Honda Research Institute (HRI-US).
